A project that uses data gathered from spaceX about passed, upcoming and current launches and all the info they have available.

Coming online Dec 19th, get notified when we launch:

A great way for fans to count down to the second when the next SpaceX rocket will launch, Live to follow on the site. With some extra info about the launch and payload. And when the countdown is close you can simply click a link to watch the livestream.

Explore every launch since the start of SpaceX and read the mission details on what went wrong or what went exactly right. With an accurate representation of the used rocket on desktop. Along with some pictures of the launch and a link to the video footage.
